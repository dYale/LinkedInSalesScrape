LinkedIn Sales Nav Scraper
Options


This extension scans several pages of the LinkedIn Sales Navigator to generate a list of emails and names that can be pasted directly into an excel spreadsheet.
Instructions
  1. Log into LinkedIn sales navigator.
  2. Search for leads through the "Search for people and companies" search bar - include any desired filters.
  3. When on the results page, fill in this extension form
  4. Words and Phrases are character and words that will match the company name shown in bold on each lead. You may enter several phrases separated by a space.
  5. Email Domain will add the email suffix to each lead.
  6. Location is optional and must match perfectly to qualify as a match
  7. Number of Pages will go the next number of requested pages and repeat the process.
  8. Email Format is the format by which we want the lead's name rendered in the generated list.
  9. Press Scrape and watch the extension cycle through the pages.
  10. When notified, open Excel and press control + v on Windows or command + v on Mac
  11. Profit
